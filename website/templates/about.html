Passions and Hobbies

My greatest passion is to create something useful and share it with others so that they can use and experience it. Seeing other people make use of something you create is like satisfying the needs or wants of that person, whether it is for entertainment or some kind of productive tool. Apart from programming, I enjoy playing chess puzzles, drawing, and reading. Chess puzzles help develop numerous skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, pattern recognition, visualization and memory which are just a few to name. These skills will prove helpful throughout my coding career. Art was one of my three main subject choices in high-school, so I believe in my artistic decision-making when designing front-end systems.

Education and Experience

I matriculated at Hyde Park High-school in 2019 with the following subjects: Maths literacy, Engineering and graphics design, Art, Geography, English and Afrikaans. In 2020, I took a gap year and the following year I decided to register for a degree in information science. At this point in my life I realized I wanted to work with computers, so I applied for informatics at UNISA and I got rejected because I lacked pure Maths in High-school. In 2022, I registered with Umalasi to write pure Maths exams at the end of the year. Having obtained pure Mathematics, I registered with UNISA and achieved one of my goals of pursuing a STEM field degree. At the end of 2022, I got a job as a retail sales assistant at Quiksilver where I worked for one year. My reason for leaving Quiksilver was to focus on my studies at university full time.
